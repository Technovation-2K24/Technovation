About


Department

The Computer Science Department at St. Claret College offers comprehensive education and training to students in information technology. The Department enriches the curriculum to meet industry requirements and prepares students for job opportunities. Classroom teaching is supplemented with specialization programs, and the experimental learning strategy, ANUSANDHAN, enhances students' confidence through experience transformation. LAKSHYA promotes research aptitude among students. The department offers extension programs, skill development workshops, peer-teaching, and e-learning activities, and collaborates with organizations to enhance students' technical skills.
